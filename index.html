EduCoin

Classroom Cryptocurrency

An idea hatched within the confines of a middle school classroom turned into a mobile responsive web application. Every school has their own in house currency, and EduCoin gives the opportunity for any teacher to have their own database, currency, and classroom economy. Students can sign in using a .stu address, while teachers can sign in using an .edu account.

The Green Tourist

Step Towards Solution

The Green Tourist is an application that gives eco-friendly tourists opportunities to give business to planet friendly businesses. Using a database of Virginia Green certified businesses the application takes a users' geolocation and provides the website and driving directions to the individual business. This allows customers to choose where their money goes as well as to help the planet one step at a time.

The State of Happiness

Find Your New

What started as a simple Google search on a prospective new living location, became an application idea to help people find their new happiness, home, job, or start. The State of Happiness gives the user specific data on a selected state. This data includes overall state mental health, average household income, social life, and population. With this data users can immediatley see which state would be good to research more, and which states should be met with caution.
